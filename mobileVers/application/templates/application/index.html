Welcome to

Get: FOCO

An all-in-one application portal to the discounted services and municipal benefits offered by the City of Fort Collins, a partnership with Fort Collins Connexion, Recreation and more.

How it Works

With Get:Foco, you’ll be able to do more with your City of Fort Collins program applications than ever before.

Control Your Application

Manage applications in real time with up-to-date application status.

Do More, Save More

If you qualify, you’ll get access to specific Connexion programs that are tailored to save you money.

Forget “Forgetting”

Never forget to renew your application ever again with easy reminders– making information updates a breeze.

Stay in the Know

Be informed with the latest news & updates from both Connexion & the City.
